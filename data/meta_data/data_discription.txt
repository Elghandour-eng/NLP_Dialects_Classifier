The data is a collection of texts in different dialects of the Arabic language.
    The texts are collected from different sources, and the dialects are classified
    into 5 categories: Gulf, Levantine, North African, Iraqi, and Egyptian. The data
    is collected from the following sources:
    1. https://www.kaggle.com/mksaad/arabic-dialects-texts
    2. https://www.kaggle.com/rtatman/blog-authorship-corpus
    3. https://www.kaggle.com/rtatman/the-billboard-200-streaming-songs-audio-features
    
    The data is collected by the following people:
    1. https://www.kaggle.com/mksaad
    2. https://www.kaggle.com/rtatman
    3. https://www.kaggle.com/dhruvildave
    
    
    The data is collected for the following purposes:
    1. https://www.kaggle.com/mksaad/arabic-dialects-texts
        - To build a model that can classify Arabic dialects.
    2. https://www.kaggle.com/rtatman/blog-authorship-corpus
        - To build a model that can classify the author of a blog post.
